Step A: 

Firstly check if IMAP is switched on

  1. Open up QQMail web client.
  2. In the top right, click Settings. 
  3. Find POP3/IMAP/SMTP/Exchange/CardDAV.
  4. Under the Start Service section, select Enable IMAP/SMTP.

Step B: Get authorization to log in to the third-party Email client

  1. Click Generate Authorization Code. 
  2. Send SMS following the guide.
  3. Confirm SMS is sent by clicking Sent.
  4. Get the Authorization Code. 
  5. Fill in QQ Email address and the Authorization Code for password.
