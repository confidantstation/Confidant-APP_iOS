Step A: 

Firstly check if IMAP is switched on

  1. Open up 163Mail web client. 
  2. In the top right, click Settings. 
  3. Select the POP3/SMTP/IMAP tab. 
  4. Under the POP3/SMTP/IMAP section, select  Enable IMAP/SMTP. 
  5. Click Save to save the changes.

Step B: Get authorization to log in to the third-party Email client

  1. Pass the security verification.  
  2. Set up your authorization code. 
  3. Click Confirm to gain your password. 
  4. Enter your Email address and password to log in.
