Step A: Find your username 

Your full iCloud email address (for example, emilyparker@icloud.com, not emilyparker) 


Step A: Find your username 

  1. Sign in to your Apple ID account.
  2. Under the Security section, below App-Specific Passwords, click on Generate Password.
  3. Follow the guide to gain your password.
  4. After you generate your app-specific password, enter or paste it into the password field of Confidant.
  5. Enter your Email address and password to log in.
