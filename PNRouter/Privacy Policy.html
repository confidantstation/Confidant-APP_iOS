Privacy Policy

Confidant utilizes state-of-the-art security and end-to-end encryption to provide private messaging and Internet calling services to users (“Services”). Your messages and files are always encrypted, so they can never be shared or viewed by anyone but yourself and the intended recipients.

Information you provide

Account Information

To create an account you shall register for our Services by setting a username. NO personal information such as phone number, or email address is required. 


Messages

Your Confidant Station is the private cloud that stores your account data including username and avatar, messages, transferred files and all the personal data. No other ones can decrypt or otherwise access the content of your messages or files. In addition, your account public/private key is stored locally. 


Emails

Confidant email client module aggregates multiple email accounts across various email services, including Gmail, Outlook, Exchange, iCloud,163 mail, Tencent Enterprise Email, QQ Email and other IMAP email clients. It supports email with its settings and all data in transmission and storage with high-level encryption between Confidant users. Encrypted email transfer is enabled between Confidant users who have their email accounts configured to the one same Confidant Station. All email account configuration settings, email content, and their associated data via Confidant are transmitted and stored with encryption.


Information you may provide

Photos, videos,chat records, contacts and SMS

Your files including photos, videos, chat records, contacts, and SMS are encrypted when they are transferred and when they are stored on Confidant hardwares which are owned by users. You can easily add items from mobile local storage to corresponding folders in Confidant if you need. Anything you sync to Confidant Encrypted Vault is automatically end-to-end encrypted. Because of the innovative hardware-software solution design, you can access to ultimate secure Node backup which means to transfer the encrypted files including photos, videos, chat records, contacts, and SMS to your Confidant hardware. The Confidant hardware owner is the only one who has the full access to all encrypted files and can decrypt them.
