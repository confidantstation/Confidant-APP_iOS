Confidant is designed to be a decentralized privacy management platform and trusted privacy-focused communications tool. Confidant messages, files and any other user personal data cannot be accessed by us or other third parties because they are always transmitted through peer-to-peer network, end-to-end encrypted and stored in the private cloud securely. Our Privacy Policy is available below.

 

Privacy Policy

Confidant utilizes state-of-the-art security and end-to-end encryption to provide private messaging and Internet calling services to users (“Services”). Your messages and files are always encrypted, so they can never be shared or viewed by anyone but yourself and the intended recipients.

 

Information you provide

Account Information

To create an account you shall register for our Services by setting a username. NO personal information such as phone number, or email address is required.

 

Messages

Your Confidant Station is the private cloud that stores your account data including username and avatar, messages, transferred files and all the personal data. No other ones can decrypt or otherwise access the content of your messages or files.

In addition, your account public/private key is stored locally.

Contacts

As the Confidant Station Owner, you can create and issue your accounts to add new members to your circle. As an ordinary Confidant user, to join a circle, you will have to receive an account QR code to become a circle member and access the owner’s Confidant Station.

 

Managing your information

You can manage your personal information in Confidant’s application Settings.
