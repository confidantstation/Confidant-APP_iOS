Step A: 

Firstly check if IMAP is switched on

  1. Open up Gmail web client.  
  2. In the top right, click Settings.
  3. Select the Forwarding and POP/IMAP tab. 
  4. Under the IMAP access section, select Enable IMAP.
  5. Click Save to save the changes.

Step B: Allow less secure apps

  1. In the top right, click Account. 
  2. Click Security in the left bar.
  3. Switch on Less secure app access. 
  4. Open up the alert email sent by Google, Confirm and Turn on activity.
  5. Enter your Email address and password to log in.
